Notes:

The equation below is derived by integrating the CTF over a range of defocus values to account for the effect of sample thickness. It can be interpreted as the classical CTF function modulated by a sinc function, and it applies to both 1D and 2D CTF cases.
Equation used:
CTF(f) = -sinc(πλf² · thickness / 2) · sin(γ(f) + arcsin(Ac))
